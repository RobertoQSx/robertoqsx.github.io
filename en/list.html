A List of Fiction

This is a list of works of fiction that I have read, am reading, or plan on doing so. The scope is limited to science fiction and fantasy. I will be updating it as I finish what is in progress, as I continue to remember works I had not included yet, or as I become aware of other ones. All the titles are in English, regardless of which of these works I have read in Spanish, or in English, or in both. Click on the tables' headers to sort the contents.
