About Roberto Q. S.



   





I am an autonomous translator. My native language is Spanish and I have bilingual fluency in English, both spoken and written. You are welcome to peruse my portfolio in this personal website and in Sigle. You can email me in order to coordinate a verbal conversation via Telegram or Signal, as well as for further communication.

This website is regularly updated. My thanks to Divya Ranjan for his guidance on HTML and for having taught me how to use GitHub Pages, where this website is mirrored. The CSS used here is ‘Simple.css’, created by Kev Quirk.

Concerning my translation journey, I would also like to thank Forrest Cameranesi and Herries Anderton for the opportunity to further develop my literary portfolio by translating their wonderful work, which is an ongoing activity.

As for creative writing, there is a fertile ground to be cherished: the “leaf-mould of the mind”, in Tolkien's words, the fruits of which I progressively reap across time. It's moss growing on a monolith. My original work is either in Spanish or in English, depending on topic (along with planned corresponding translations), and I have dabbled with doing so in French. Every instance of indulging in creative writing is an enriching and enjoyable endeavour.
